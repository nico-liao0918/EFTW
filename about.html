What is EFV/T?

The EFV/T stands for Eco-Friendly Vehicle/Technology.

This is the next generation transportation concept that is powered by a closed-loop, water-based propolsion system. Unlike traditional combustion or electric or hybrid vehicles, the EFV operates independently of any external fuel or charging stations. It is a unique design that utilizes pressurized water to generate motion and electricity -- all within a zero-emission, self-sustaining loop.

It can be used interchangeably. Using EFV or EFT. Either way is ok.

Our Mission

We aim to redifine sustainability in the automotive industry by introducing a clean, scalable, and innovative approach to vehicle energy. The EFV is designed to be accessible, practical, and adaptable -- capable of serving personal cars, buses, trucks, and future utility vehciles with minimal environment impact.

Vision Statement

We envision the world where vehicles no longer pollute or depend on limited energy infrastructure. The EFV/T is a step toward that future -- a future where water, pressure, and innovation work together to create a truly green transportation system.

Our Story

This project began with a question, "what if there is no need for refueling or charging a vehicle, will it truly be green solutins?" This question led us to draft a theory in paper before we have written our first technical paper, our white paper. This was a culmination of those theory, research, time, and effort in this EFV. While this is still in the early stages, the vision is intended to challenge current standards and attract potential partnership and cooperation toward innovation and sustainability.
